The Ultimate Guide to Shoe Rotation for Runners

Rotating between different shoe types can reduce repetitive strain, improve durability per pair and enhance training quality. Here is how to build rotations for common goals and weekly volumes.

Why rotate?

  • Vary loading: Alternating cushioning levels and rocker profiles changes stress patterns.
  • Extend lifespan: Midsoles decompress between sessions, maintaining resilience.
  • Purposebuilt sessions: Cushioned trainers for easy days; snappier shoes for workouts.

Example rotations

  • 3day runner: Daily trainer + cushioned longrun shoe.
  • 5day runner: Daily trainer + tempo/interval shoe + longrun cushion.
  • Marathon block: Daily trainer + plated tempo shoe + race shoe for key workouts.
