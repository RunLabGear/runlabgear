Top 7 Stability Running Shoes for Overpronators (2025)

Modern stability shoes guide motion without the rigid posts of older designs. If you visibly overpronate or get recurring shin, knee or arch issues, these models provide subtle support while maintaining a smooth, modern ride.

  • ASICS Gel‑Kayano 31: Plush guidance and broad fit — dependable for high mileage. ~£180. Buy
  • Brooks Adrenaline GTS 24: GuideRails alignment without intrusion. ~£150. Buy
  • Saucony Guide 17: Stable platform and comfortable upper; good value. ~£135. Buy
  • HOKA Arahi 7: Lightweight stability with a rolling rocker. ~£130. Buy

Do you need stability?

Check wear patterns and consider a gait assessment if unsure. If you are injury‑free in neutral shoes, there is no need to switch. Choose stability only when there’s a clear benefit.
